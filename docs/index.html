About the App

Our app helps Boy Scouts track their progress and objectives. Easily set, manage, and achieve goals, making scouting more engaging!

Features

  • Track multiple objectives
  • Set reminders and deadlines
  • Share achievements with friends
  • Customizable goal setting
  • Progress tracking and analytics
